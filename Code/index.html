Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? We've shared some of our most frequently asked questions to help you out!

Press the large green IN button when you begin work. You can designate the time and date that you have clocked in by changing the Clock Time before pressing the IN button.

Press the large green OUT button when you finish work. You can designate the time and date that you have clocked out by changing the Clock Time before pressing the OUT button.

Click Edit/Submit Timecard to either edit your current Timecard or submit your completed Timecard.

You can change the out time and date to the previous time you actually left and missed clocking out.
